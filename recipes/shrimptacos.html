Shrimp Tacos

TACOOO TUESDAY CALLS FOR SHRIMP TACOOOOS!

Make the best shrimp tacos you've ever tasted, guranteed!

Ingredients Needed

  • shrimp
  • taco sized tortillas
  • mixed seasoning
  • olive oil
  • cilantro ranch sauce
  • salsa

Steps

  1. thaw and peel shrimp
  2. season shrimp
  3. cook shrimp
  4. grill tortillas
  5. place shrimp on tortillas
  6. add desired toppings on top
  7. ENJOY
