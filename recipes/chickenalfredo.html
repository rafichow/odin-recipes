Chicken Alfredo

YOU KNOW YOU'VE ALWAYS WANTED TO MAKE CHICKEN ALFREDO!

A super simple, but delicious meal to make with only a few ingredients!

Ingredients Needed

  • chicken
  • heavy cream
  • butter
  • fettuccine pasta

Steps

  1. boil noodles
  2. cook chicken
  3. make or use store bought alfredo sauce
  4. assemble ingredients together
