Spaghetti & Meatballs

The CLASSIC Spaghetti and Meatballs

Show you how to whip up an easy, comfort food!

Ingredients Needed

  • meatballs
  • spaghetti noodle
  • marinara sauce
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. thaw meatballs
  2. cook meatballs
  3. while cooking meatballs, add seasoning to it
  4. boil pasta noodles
  5. save one cup of pasta water
  6. drain pasta water
  7. add marinara to pasta
  8. add meatballs to pasta
  9. add cup of pasta water to the mix
  10. mix
  11. serve and enjoy
